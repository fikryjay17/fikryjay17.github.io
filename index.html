On Collage

The Indonesian Education University (UPI) as one of the Legal Entity State Universities (PTN-BH) in Indonesia is a university with an educational identity. UPI implements a multi-campus system with UPI Bandung or Bumi Siliwangi as the Central Campus, while Regional Campuses are spread across West Java Province including Cibiru, Tasikmalaya, Purwakarta, Sumedang, and Banten Province in Serang City. As a PTN, new students are accepted every year through the National Selection for State Higher Education (SNMPTN), Joint Selection for State University Admission (SBMPTN), Independent Selection, and Special Achievements.

History

Marine Information Systems Study Program

Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) as a pioneer and superior university needs to contribute in this field and also in the context of UPI as a Center of Excellence in the field of Vocational Education. Then, in order to prepare human resources for the maritime sector in the era of the industrial revolution 4.0, UPI in 2019 plans to open a Marine Information Systems study program.

SMarine Information System is a part of Marine Science which focuses on analyzing marine data both numerically and spatially to be integrated into an information system that is developed as a basis for decision making. Skilled human resources in this regard are indispensable in development in the maritime sector in industry 4.0.

Vision

Develop and implement information systems to support technological developments in the marine sector and produce graduates who are competitive and skilled in processing numerical and spatial data.

Mission

  • Organizing national standard education and teaching services in the field of marine information system expertise,
  • Carry out research in the field of marine information systems and innovations in an effort to develop the potential of coastal and marine biological resources at the regional and national level,
  • Applying integrated, productive and measurable marine information technology in education, research, and community service and empowerment based on research results.
  • Develop and disseminate science and technology in the marine sector based on the needs of the community and industry.

Accreditation

Accreditation is an assessment activity to determine the feasibility of Study Programs and Higher Education. Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) is one of the State Universities in Indonesia which was founded in 1954. UPI started as a Teacher Education College which has undergone a metamorphosis to become one of the State Universities with Legal Entities in Indonesia with University A Accreditation.
